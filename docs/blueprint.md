# **App Name**: AgentiveAI

## Core Features:

- Profile Display: Display Kanan Vyas's profile information including name, title, tagline, location, and a brief about me section.
- Experience Timeline: Showcase professional experience in a timeline format, highlighting roles, companies, periods, and descriptions.
- Blog Showcase: Feature a selection of blog posts with titles, dates, excerpts, and links to the full articles on Medium.
- Theme Toggle: Enable users to switch between a light and dark theme for optimal viewing experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to reflect expertise in AI with a touch of sophistication.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F0F0F0) for the light mode and dark charcoal (#333333) for the dark mode, providing a clean and contrasting backdrop.
- Accent color: Electric Blue (#7DF9FF) for interactive elements and highlights to draw attention and reflect the tech-forward nature of the portfolio.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font known for its readability and modern aesthetic, ensuring a seamless reading experience.
- Use icons from Lucide-React for a consistent and professional visual language.
- Maintain a clean, well-spaced layout with a maximum width for readability and a focus on content hierarchy.
- Implement subtle animations for transitions and hover effects to enhance interactivity without being distracting.
